Meme

Korean Cho Hye Lyun released Tae Bo diet class videos called "Cho Hye Lyun's Taebo Classes" in 2003 and 2006. It wasn't very popular at the time, but the cartoonist Lee Sang-Il and members from DCinside made it popular in 2019. Using the DVD videos of the Tae Bo Classes, video uploader 'Mesenghe' released 'Cho Hye Lyun and Tae Bo's Curse' on August 8th, 2018. This was a remix between "Cho Hye Lyun's Taebo Classes" and "The Nightmare Woods" from the game Shantae and the Pirate's Curse. The video is subtitled in more than 190 languages, becoming the most subtitled video on Youtube all over the world.
